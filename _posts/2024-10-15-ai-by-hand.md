# AI by hand

## Introduction
Generally I'm not too keen on social media, even though I have benefited and continue to benefit from extroverted people who have made peace with [personal branding](https://rachel.fast.ai/posts/2017-12-18-personal-brand/index.html). Some weeks ago, I stumbled across Prof. Tom Yeh who teaches [AI by hand](https://aibyhand.substack.com/). It's great to approach more complicated ideas using first principles, by breaking down the steps so as to gain a better handle of the topic.  
Recently, I stumbled across a LinkedIn post, presenting an [introduction to Transformers in 25 steps](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nithishyadavs31_transformers-math-by-hand-under-25-steps-activity-7251212209278656512-q4cs).
