# About

Hi there 👋! I'm Eirini, an AI Engineer / Full-stack Data Scientist in the UK and a proponent of [Just-in-Time learning](https://xcancel.com/radekosmulski/status/1828564396265926967).   
Favouring a minimalist approach, to produce sustainable[^1] and robust software, I believe that effective computing is accomplished through humility, simplicity, efficiency, streamlined processes, and unified workflows.  

Currently I'm betting on [Deno](https://deno.com/), while keeping an eye on [Elixir](https://elixir-lang.org/) and [Nx](https://github.com/elixir-nx). This unusual choice aims at teaching me new ways of thinking, [questioning the dominance of Python](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qvieJBbUuDk) in mainstream Data Science.

My GitHub handle was inspired by Chris Molnar's excellent short read [Modeling Mindsets](https://ai-mindset.github.io/).

I choose not to use Twitter / X. You can find me on [📣 [in]](https://www.linkedin.com/in/eirinikomninou)
