# About

Hi there 👋! I'm Eirini, an AI Engineer / Full-stack Data Scientist in the UK and a proponent of [Just-in-Time learning](https://xcancel.com/radekosmulski/status/1828564396265926967) -to quote a [successful](https://radekosmulski.com/about/), [meta learning](https://rosmulski.gumroad.com/l/learn_machine_learning), [fast.ai](https://course.fast.ai/) alumnus.
